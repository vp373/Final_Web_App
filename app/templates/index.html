History of Old Faithful

Old Faithful is one of over 500 geysers at Yellowstone National Park in Wyoming. Since 2000, Old faithful has erupted consistently every 45 mins to 2 hours! These eruptions range in height anywhere from 100 ft to nearly 200 ft!

This applications allows you to see some data behind Old Faithful's eruptions. In the charts tab, you can see a comparison of how long the geyser erupts for vs how long in between each eruption. Below you will find a table with more than 250 data points!
